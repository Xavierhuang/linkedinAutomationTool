## Tips for Best Results

### Material Selection

**Do Include:**
- Recent content (last 1-2 years)
- Clear brand messaging
- Target audience information
- Value propositions
- Customer success stories

**Avoid:**
- Legal documents (privacy policies)
- Technical documentation
- Internal-only materials
- Outdated content

### Optimal Material Mix

- **Website**: Homepage + About + 1-2 key pages
- **Blog**: 3-5 recent posts
- **Documents**: 1-2 PDFs (brochures, presentations)
- **Images**: 2-3 brand assets (optional)

### Campaign Customization

After generation, you can:
- Edit target audience
- Adjust content pillars
- Change posting frequency
- Modify tone and voice
- Update image settings
